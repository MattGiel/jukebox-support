Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 30, 2025

Overview

JukeBox respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Basic app usage data (e.g., events like creating a room or joining a session).
  • Apple Music–related permissions you grant to enable playback during group sessions.

How We Use Information

  • Operate core features such as creating rooms and turn-based playback.
  • Improve reliability and troubleshoot issues.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share minimal data with service providers only to operate the app (for example, analytics or error reporting), under appropriate safeguards.

Your Choices

  • You can revoke Apple Music permissions at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can request deletion of support correspondence at any time.
